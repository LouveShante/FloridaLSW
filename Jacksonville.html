Welcome to Jacksonville

Jacksonville, located in Duval county, is the largest city by land mass in the United States. Jacksonville is also home to the Dames Point Bridge that opened in 1989 and is the second longest cable bridge.



There are many activities to do here no matter the age. We have the Zoo & Gardens that features 2,000 animals, The Cummer Museum, and the Florida Theartre. If you enjoy attending events, be sure to check out a Jaguars football game at the TIAA EverBank Stadium, a Jumbo Shrimp baseball game at the 121 Financial Ballpark, or show at the Vystar Veterans Memorial Arena.

Beaches

Jacksonville Beach, Neptune Beach, Mayport Beach, and Atlantic Beach are the cities main beaches and are public and pet friendly. In the Northside of Jax there are Islands such as, Blackrock Beach, Boneyard Beach, Little Talbot Island, and Hugenot Park. The beaches offer restraunts, beach boutiques, and surf shops with free and paid parking.
